Igala Digital Liberating and Digitizing Igala's Content for good.

IgalaDigital is a people-driven non-profit empowering active citizentry and civic watchdogs to help keep and build Igala's Data and Content digital/Open.

About Us

How We Do This

Data Liberation

Turning deadwood paper archives into a digital resource. Hosting Data liberation scraperthons to unlock knowledge held in academia and citizen groups.

Data Tools

We supports the development of citizen driven data solutions and champions its reuse wherever appropriate.

Data Fellowship

Each fellow builds proof-of-concept services, helps to develop internal policy, data platforms and recruitment/staff strategies.

Data Skills

We bring in data pioneers such as International Center for Journalists, Google, Hacks/Hackers to synchronize and amplify their programs.
